Privacy Notice

Sparked Connection Ltd — Last updated April 2026

1. Who We Are

Sparked Connection Ltd ("we", "us", "our") provides a modern, personalised matchmaking service for professionals.

We act as the data controller for your personal data under UK data protection law.

Contact: hello@sparkedconnection.com

2. Our Approach to Privacy

Matchmaking is personal. We treat your information with care, discretion, and confidentiality.

We only collect and use data that is necessary to understand you, assess compatibility, and introduce you thoughtfully to others.

Important We do not operate like dating apps, and your data is never used for mass matching or algorithmic exposure.

3. What Personal Data We Collect

Information you provide

  • Name, email address, contact details
  • Age or date of birth
  • Location (city/region)
  • Relationship preferences and intentions
  • Lifestyle information (interests, habits, values)
  • Personality assessment responses
  • Photographs (where provided)
  • Messages or communications with us

Sensitive / special category data

Some information (e.g. personal values, beliefs, lifestyle preferences) may be considered special category data. We only process this data with your explicit consent, where it is necessary for matchmaking, and with appropriate safeguards in place.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Assess your suitability for matchmaking
  • Understand compatibility and preferences
  • Provide curated, considered introductions
  • Communicate with you about your membership
  • Manage payments and service access
  • Improve our services
Your data is used only for the purpose of providing a personalised matchmaking service.

5. AI-Assisted Analysis & Human Oversight

We may use secure, private AI-assisted tools to support analysing questionnaire responses, identifying compatibility patterns, and improving matching insight.

Human-first approach All decisions are reviewed by a human matchmaker. No decisions are made solely by automated means. AI supports — it does not replace — human judgement.

This ensures a considered, personal approach at every stage.

6. Lawful Basis for Processing

We rely on the following legal bases:

  • Contract – to provide matchmaking services you request
  • Legitimate interests – to assess compatibility and operate responsibly
  • Consent – where required (e.g. optional communications, sensitive data)
  • Legal obligations – for accounting or regulatory purposes

You may withdraw consent at any time where applicable.

7. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or distribute your data.

With potential matches

As part of the matchmaking process, we may share limited profile information, such as first name or initial, general location, profession or lifestyle overview, selected photographs, and high-level preferences.

Important We only share information necessary to assess mutual interest. Identifiable details (e.g. contact information) are only shared after both parties agree to proceed. Photographs or detailed profiles are shared only with your explicit consent.

With service providers

We may use trusted third parties for website hosting, payment processing, communication tools, and secure data handling. All providers are subject to data protection agreements.

8. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not publicly display your profile
  • We do not allow browsing or swiping
  • We do not sell your data
  • We do not make automated matchmaking decisions

9. International Data Transfers

Where data is processed outside the UK, we ensure appropriate safeguards, such as UK adequacy regulations and standard contractual clauses.

10. Data Storage & Security

We use appropriate technical and organisational measures, including restricted access controls, secure systems and environments, and confidentiality safeguards. Your data is accessed only where necessary.

11. Data Retention

  • Active clients: for the duration of membership
  • Inactive applications: limited retention for review
  • Legal/accounting data: as required by law

Photographs and personal profiles can be deleted upon request.

12. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access your personal data, correct inaccurate data, request deletion, restrict or object to processing, request data portability, and withdraw consent where applicable.

To exercise your rights, contact us at hello@sparkedconnection.com

13. Complaints

If you have concerns, please contact us first. You may also contact the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

14. Cookies & Website Analytics

Our website may use cookies or analytics tools to understand website usage and improve performance. Where required, we will obtain your consent before placing non-essential cookies.

15. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time. The latest version will always be available on our website.

Matchmaking relies on trust.
We are committed to handling your information with the same care, discretion, and respect that we bring to every introduction.
