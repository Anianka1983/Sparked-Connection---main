About Matchmaking

Modern matchmaking for professionals seeking meaningful relationships.

What is Sparked Connection?

Sparked Connection is a London-based matchmaking service for working professionals over the age of 25 who are looking for meaningful relationships.

The service combines personality psychology, AI compatibility analysis, and human matchmaking expertise.

Who is it for?

  • Professionals
  • People tired of dating apps
  • Individuals seeking long-term relationships

How does it work?

  • Personality assessment (Big Five, MBTI, Love Languages)
  • AI compatibility matching
  • Matchmaker review
  • Introductions
  • Curated events
