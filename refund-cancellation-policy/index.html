1. Overview

This Refund & Cancellation Policy explains your rights and our approach to cancellations and refunds when using Sparked Connection's matchmaking services.

Sparked Connection provides a bespoke, personalised matchmaking service, involving time, expertise, and human judgement. As such, refund rights are limited once the service has begun.

2. Membership Tiers & Fees

Sparked Connection offers three membership tiers, each with different terms:

  • Bronze (Free) — no fee is charged; no refund applies
  • Gold (£800) — 12-month active membership with a minimum of 5 Agreed Introductions
  • Platinum (£1,500) — two simultaneous 12-month Gold memberships, each entitled to a minimum of 5 Agreed Introductions, with priority matching and a dedicated matchmaker

Fees for Gold and Platinum memberships are payable in full in advance.

3. Cooling-Off Period (Consumer Rights)

If you purchase a membership online or at a distance, you may have the right to cancel within 14 days under UK consumer law.

Important By requesting that matchmaking services begin within the 14-day cooling-off period, you expressly acknowledge and agree that your right to cancel will be lost once the service has commenced.

This does not affect your statutory rights where applicable.

4. When the Service Begins

For the purposes of cancellations and refunds, the service is deemed to commence only once the scheduled 1:1 consultation (in-person or online) has taken place following contract signature.

Prior to this consultation, Sparked Connection may carry out administrative onboarding activities, including reviewing submitted information or preparing for the consultation. These activities do not constitute commencement of the matchmaking service.

Important Once the 1:1 consultation has taken place, the service is considered to have formally commenced, and the provisions relating to cancellation and refunds after commencement will apply. Where a Client delays or fails to attend the scheduled consultation without reasonable notice, Sparked Connection reserves the right to treat the service as commenced after a 3-week period.”

5. Refunds After Service Commencement

Once the service has commenced, membership fees are non-refundable except where required by UK law. This reflects the time, expertise, and personalised nature of the service.

Dissatisfaction with introductions or outcomes does not entitle the Client to a refund where the service has been delivered as described.

6. Introduction Guarantee & Extensions

Gold and Platinum members are entitled to a minimum of 5 Agreed Introductions over their 12-month Membership Period.

If the minimum of 5 Agreed Introductions has not been fulfilled by the end of the Membership Period through no fault of the Client, Sparked Connection will extend the membership at no additional cost until the minimum is met.

The extension applies only where:

  • The Client has actively engaged with the process and responded to communications
  • Delays are attributable to match availability or operational reasons, not Client non-participation
Please note Bronze members are part of our Singles Network but are not entitled to a guaranteed minimum number of introductions. Unused introductions do not entitle any Client to a refund.

7. Cancellation by the Client

You may cancel your membership at any time by contacting us in writing. Upon cancellation, matchmaking activity will cease and fees already paid remain non-refundable once the service has commenced.

Where applicable, Sparked Connection may offer a reasonable extension of the service instead of a refund, subject to availability and cooperation.

8. Client Participation & Responsibility

The effectiveness of the service depends on your active engagement, availability, and responsiveness. Where a Client becomes unresponsive or unavailable for over 90 calendar days, the matchmaking process may be paused.

Any period of non-responsiveness, non-engagement, or unavailability by the Client may not count toward the 12-month Membership Period for the purposes of the introduction guarantee and does not entitle the Client to a refund or extension.

9. Suspension or Termination by Sparked Connection

We reserve the right to suspend or terminate membership immediately where a Client provides false, inaccurate, incomplete, or misleading information; breaches any eligibility representation, warranty, or undertaking given to Sparked Connection; behaves in a manner that is inappropriate, unsafe, disrespectful, threatening, abusive, or inconsistent with the values of the service; misuses introductions; or becomes repeatedly non-responsive without reasonable explanation.

Please note In such circumstances, membership fees are non-refundable, and any outstanding service obligations, including unused Agreed Introductions, may at our discretion be treated as fulfilled.

You acknowledge that Sparked Connection relies on the accuracy of the information, representations, and warranties you provide when accepting you into membership and delivering a personalised matchmaking service.

10. Exceptional Circumstances

We may consider partial refunds or alternative arrangements in exceptional circumstances. Any such decision is made at our sole discretion and does not create a precedent.

11. Statutory Rights

Nothing in this policy affects your statutory rights under UK consumer law.
