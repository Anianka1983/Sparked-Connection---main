4. Definitions

"Agreed Introduction"

An introduction where both parties have confirmed willingness to be introduced and to receive each other's profile or contact details.

"Membership Period"

The 12-month period beginning on the date of acceptance or purchase of a Gold or Platinum membership.

"Singles Network"

The pool of Bronze members whose profiles are held on file and made available to our matchmakers for consideration in future introductions.

"Member Event"

Any social event, networking event, workshop, matchmaking event, gathering, online event, or other activity organised by Sparked Connection from time to time.

"Second Match Promise"

A flexible membership option (£150) available when two Bronze members are identified as a match. Only one person needs to join and is guaranteed a second introduction if the first match is not the right fit. No fixed time limit applies.

Where two Bronze (free) members are identified as a compatible match, either party may opt for the Second Match Promise membership (£150). Under this arrangement:

Bronze members are part of our Singles Network but are not entitled to a guaranteed minimum number of introductions unless they have purchased the Second Match Promise.

Following the introduction, the joining (paying) member must confirm the outcome to Sparked Connection within 2 days of the date taking place, indicating whether the match was successful or unsuccessful.

Important

This confirmation determines eligibility for the guaranteed second introduction. Where confirmation is not received within the 2-day window, Sparked Connection reserves the right to treat the match as successful for the purposes of this Section, and the guaranteed second introduction will not apply.
